Welcome to TOAST

This is a game of mental thinking. Here are the rules
  1. Everyone gets a piece of paper/table to draw on.
  2. Each person gets 5 minutes to draw, on how they make toast.
  3. Once everyone has completed, show to each other and have a laugh :)
  4. There is no wrong answer to this. Good Luck!
